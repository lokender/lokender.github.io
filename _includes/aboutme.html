I am a 5th year Ph.D. student at , IIIT-Delhi . I work in the domain of Computer Vision and Deep Learning. I am advised by Dr. Saket Anand . My Ph.D. is supported by Visvesvarya Ph.D. Fellowship, Govt. of India .


My Ph.D. research work focuses on robust statistical methods for learning and vision.

Research Interests:
  • Robust Geometric Computer Vision (2D and 3D)
  • Deep Learning For Geometric Computer Vision
  • Robust Statistical Methods For Robust Deep Learning
  • Image Based 3D Modeling
  • Computer Vision for Autonomous Driving
