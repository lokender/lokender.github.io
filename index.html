Dr. Lokender Tiwari, PhD

Research Scientist at TCS Research

                 

I am a research scientist at TCS Research, where I work on Computer Vision, Graphics and Deep Learning. Prior to this, I was working at NEC Laboratories America, with Prof. Manmohan Chandraker, Dr. Quoc-Huy Tran, and Dr. Pan Ji on self-improving framework for SLAM. I received my PhD from IIIT-Delhi, where I was advised by Dr. Saket Anand and funded by the Visvesvarya PhD fellowship. I am the recipient of Chancellor's Gold Medal for academic excellence in B.Tech (Computer Science and Engineering) 2008-2012.

Research : I actively research in the follwoing domain areas: computer vision (2D/3D), computer graphics, machine vision and robust learning.

Application Areas : I am interested in following application areas:
  • Autonomous Driving (Scene Understanding, SLAM etc)
  • Metaverse (3D World, Virtual Avatars, Telepresence, Activity Transfer)
  • Creative Content Creation (Synthesis and Evaluation)
  • Human Motion Capture and Transfer
  • Human Robot Interaction
  • Robust and Reliable Deep Learning
